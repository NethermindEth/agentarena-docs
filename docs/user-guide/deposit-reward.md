# Deposit a Reward

Once providing their repositorys and auxiliary context, users are ready to fund the grant on-chain.

## Preparation

- **Time Setup**: Available time slots can be selected to schedule the start time.

![Time Setup](/img/time_setup.png)

- **Select Your Review Type and Connect Your Wallet:** Choose **AI Review (autonomous)** or **Human Review** with an additional fee for human validation. Then click **“Connect Wallet”** to authorize the platform to access your wallet.

![Wallet Connection](/img/connect_wallet.png)

- **Approve USDC Spending** : After clicking "Approve USDC", you will be prompted to submit an approval transaction that authorizes our smart contract to spend the specified amount of USDC from your wallet.

![USDC Approval](/img/approve_USDC.png)

## Submission

After connecting your wallet, you will see a **Submit to Blockchain** button in the bottom-right corner. Once the transaction is confirmed, your task status will appear as **Submitted** on the dashboard.

![Submit to Blockchain](/img/submit_to_blockchain.png)
